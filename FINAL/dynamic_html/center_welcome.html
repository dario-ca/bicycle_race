Welcome,

this application is designed to help you analyze the DiVVy-Bike data.

The application relies on data officially released by DiVVy and includes almost 760,000 rides through Chicago from June to December 2013.

To start visualizing data, just simply use the interactive map at the left and the graph and filter menu at the right. You can adjust the filters to your needs.
